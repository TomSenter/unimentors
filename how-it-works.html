What is a client?

When you sign up to learn with our tutors, you sign up as a client, a client can have multiple student accounts under it, and will deal with all the payments for those students etc. Once you've made an account with Unimentors, we will automatically make a student account, and connect it for you. This is where the learning takes place. If you request a tutor on the find a tutor page, you will automatically be made a student account, and we will link that to your a client account, to handle all the payments etc.
