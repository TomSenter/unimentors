Privacy policy

Unimentors Privacy Policy All data that users input to unimentors.co.uk will be stored securely centrally on Heroku servers that operate near Dublin (fully GDPR compliant). They are hosted by the Amazon Web Service (AWS) and they have backups on at least 2 other continents. Some data is not stored (see 1.8 and 2). The only data inputted by users accessible freely to any user that accesses the site is some of the information that ‘tutors’ (see 1.2) enter, which will always be stated to tutors as they enter the data. Stored data is deleted after 3 years. We delete our data by contacting the AWS.

1.0 Definitions
1.1 ‘User’ refers to any person using the website, whether signed-up using to any account type or not.
1.2 ‘Mentor’/’tutor’ refers to anyone that has registered and been accepted for a tutor/mentor account.
1.3 ‘Student’ refers to anyone signed up with a student account.
1.4 ‘Find tutor’ refers to the webpage in which students can search for tutors from their university.
1.5 ‘Unimentors’ refers to the website ‘‘www.unimentors.co.uk’’.
1.6 ‘Unimentors team’ refers to the persons running Unimentors.
1.7 ‘Collected data’ refers to all information that users input to Unimentors, but is not necessarily stored permanently – see 1.8.
1.8 ‘Stored data’ Is data that is stored permanently (unless a viable request to delete the data has been put forward).

3.0 What information do we collect/store? All of the following is collected and stored data unless stated otherwise:
3.1 Tutor account information: First and second names, university email address of user, home address, date of birth, optionally a mobile number or contact telephone number.

3.12 We do not collect cookies

4.0 How we get the personal information and why we have it
4.1 We collect all information by forms that the user will input data into manually. We do not automatically collect any data.
4.2 All information collected from students is due to one of the following reasons: administrational purposes from Unimentors (including payment), to improve the quality of service, to help tutors know a student’s name and photo (if the student chooses to show themselves), to check that a student or tutor of the student is not violating the websites terms and conditions (including our no collusion/plagiarism conditions).
4.3 All information collected from tutors is due to one of the following reasons: administrational purposes from Unimentors (including receiving payment), to improve the quality of service, to help students decide if a tutor is well suited to them , to check that a tutor or student of the tutor is not violating the websites terms and conditions (including our no collusion/plagiarism conditions).

5.0 We do not share your personal data with anyone except for tutorcruncher, the software we use to run our website. They do not share any users data with anyone.

6.0 Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the lawful bases we rely on for processing this information are:
(a) Your consent. You are able to remove your consent at any time. You can do this by contacting contact@unimentors.co.uk
(b) We have a legitimate interest.

7.0 Your data protection rights
Under data protection law, you have rights including:
Your right of access - You have the right to ask us for copies of your personal information.
Your right to rectification - You have the right to ask us to rectify personal information you think is inaccurate. You also have the right to ask us to complete information you think is incomplete.
Your right to erasure - You have the right to ask us to erase your personal information in certain circumstances.
Your right to restriction of processing - You have the right to ask us to restrict the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.
Your right to object to processing - You have the the right to object to the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.
Your right to data portability - You have the right to ask that we transfer the personal information you gave us to another organisation, or to you, in certain circumstances.
You are not required to pay any charge for exercising your rights. If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you.
Please contact us at contact@unimentors.co.uk if you wish to make a request.

8.0 How to complain
If you have any concerns about our use of your personal information, you can make a complaint to us at contact@unimentors.co.uk.
You can also complain to the ICO if you are unhappy with how we have used your data.
